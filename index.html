About PiSun

PiSun is a solar-powered water purification system designed to provide clean, potable water using sunlight. Our mission is to make clean water a right, not a privilege, for every community in South Sudan.

How PiSun Works

  • Solar PV Panels: Generate electricity from sunlight.
  • Advanced Filtration: Removes bacteria, viruses, and sediments.
  • Scalable Design: Suitable for households or entire communities.

Real-World Impact

In Norbor village, PiSun has provided clean water to over 500 residents. Waterborne diseases have dropped significantly, and overall health has improved.
